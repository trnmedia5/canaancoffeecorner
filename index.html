Canaan Coffee Corner

A church coffee ministry created to provide fellowship before Sunday School while fundraising to support Canaan Teens.

Our Mission

Canaan Coffee Corner exists to serve our church family by offering a welcoming space for connection on Sunday mornings while helping fund youth ministry.

$500+ Raised

Since launching this ministry, profits from every cup have gone directly toward supporting Canaan Teens through events, trips, and ministry opportunities.
